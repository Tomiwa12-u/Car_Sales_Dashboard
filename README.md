# Car Sales Analysis Dashboard
## Project Overview
This project is an interactive Excel dashboard developed to analyze car sales performance and customer demographics. The dashboard transforms raw sales data into meaningful insights, enabling stakeholders to monitor key business metrics, understand customer purchasing behavior, and identify sales trends.

The project demonstrates my ability to clean, transform, analyze, and visualize data using Microsoft Excel, Power Query, Pivot Tables, and Dashboard Design techniques.
## Project Access 
[Check out my work here](Car_sales_dashboard)

## Business Objectives
The objectives of this analysis were to:

* Monitor overall sales performance and revenue generation.
* Identify top-performing vehicle brands.
* Analyze customer demographics and purchasing patterns.
* Evaluate sales distribution across gender groups.
* Examine customer salary segments and their contribution to sales.
* Track weekly and monthly sales trends.
* Provide an interactive dashboard for data-driven decision-making.

## Key Insights
* Chevrolet recorded the highest sales quantity among all vehicle brands.
* Male customers accounted for the majority of sales transactions.
* Customers within the highest salary group contributed significantly to total sales volume.
* Monthly sales displayed an upward trend toward the end of the year.
* A small group of customers generated a large portion of total sales quantity.
